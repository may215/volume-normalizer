
# App Icon & Screenshot Specifications

Prepare these assets for your Google Play Store listing.

## 1. App Icon
-   **Format**: 32-bit PNG (with alpha).
-   **Dimensions**: 512px x 512px.
-   **File Size**: Max 1024KB.
-   **Safe Zone**: Keep important logos within the center 384x384 area (Google applies a mask).
-   **Style**: Flat, distinct, no shadows (Google adds shadow).

## 2. Feature Graphic
-   **Usage**: Displayed at top of store listing and in promotions.
-   **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha).
-   **Dimensions**: 1024px x 500px.
-   **Content**: Branding artwork only. Avoid text unless it's the logo. Keep critical elements centered.

## 3. Screenshots
You need at least **2 screenshots**. Recommendation: 4-5 showing core workflow.

### Phone (Portrait)
-   **Ratio**: 9:16 (typical).
-   **Min Dimension**: 320px.
-   **Max Dimension**: 3840px.
-   **Recommendation**: 1080 x 1920 px.

### 7-inch Tablet
-   **Required**: At least 1 screenshot if you want "Designed for Tablets" designation.
-   **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG.
-   **Recommendation**: 1200 x 1920 px (Portrait) or 1920 x 1200 px (Landscape).

### 10-inch Tablet
-   **Required**: At least 1 screenshot.
-   **Recommendation**: 1600 x 2560 px (Portrait) or 2560 x 1600 px (Landscape).
